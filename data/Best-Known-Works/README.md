## Priority Translation Targets

Based on significance and accessibility gaps:

**The Bab (Critical):**
- Persian Bayan (major doctrinal work)
- Arabic Bayan (major doctrinal work)
- Qayyumu'l-Asma' (complete, not just excerpts)

**Baha'u'llah (High Priority):**
- Kitab-i-Badi' (major apologetic text)
- Qasidiy-i-Varqa'iyyih (major poem)
- Lawh-i-Huriyyih (Tablet of the Maiden)
- Lawh-i-Ruh (Tablet of Spirit)
- Lawh-i-Ru'ya (Tablet of Vision)
- Lawh-i-Kullu't-Ta'am (only provisional exists)
- Complete Suriy series (50+ untranslated)

**'Abdu'l-Baha (High Priority):**
- Tafsir-i-Kuntu Kanzan Makhfiyya (doctrinal significance)
- Lawh-i-Muhabbat (Tablet of Love)
- Lawh-i-Tarbiyat (Tablet of Education)

**Shoghi Effendi:**
- All 8 Tawqi'at including the Lawh-i-Qarn (Century Message, 1944) in both Arabic and Persian
